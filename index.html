How big the problem is

Mental health affects millions worldwide these numbers aren’t just stats, they’re real lives.

4 B

Approximately 50% of the global population, or 4 billion people, will experience a mental health disorder in their lifetime. However, less than 50% receive necessary care.

Reference: World Health Organization (WHO).

1 in 8

Worldwide, roughly 970 million people experience mental health conditions, with 14% of India's population affected. Suicide is the second leading cause of death among 15-29-year-olds.

References: WHO; National Mental Health Survey of India.

Understanding Mental Health

Mental health challenges affect millions, often going unnoticed. Here are some common struggles :

Anxiety & Stress – Constant worry, restlessness, and feeling overwhelmed.
Depression – Loss of interest, fatigue, and persistent sadness.
PTSD & Trauma – Flashbacks, nightmares, and emotional distress.
Bipolar Disorder – Intense mood shifts from extreme highs to deep lows.
